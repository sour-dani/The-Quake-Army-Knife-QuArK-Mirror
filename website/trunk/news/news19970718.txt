<DD><FONT SIZE=3 COLOR="#0080FF"><B>- QuArK Bugfix release -</B></FONT><P>
Armin wrote and told me about a bug in 3.61 that was introduced when putting in the new logo. It is a fairly minor bug that made the windows in the QuArK Explorer unable to function. However, since Armin couldn't do it himself because of having no 'net access, he sent me the info on how to fix it, and I patched the "quark.exe".  If you downloaded the original "quark361.zip" download <A HREF="http://www.cdrom.com/pub/idgames2/planetquake/quark/qk361exe.zip">this</A> zipfile with the patched exe file. Just replace the one in your QuArK directory with this new one. If you have not downloaded version 3.61 yet, I have replaced the full package with <A HREF="http://www.cdrom.com/pub/idgames2/planetquake/quark/qrk361p.zip">qrk361p.zip</A> which contains the full package with the fixed exe file. (If you get a "file not found" error when trying to download these, just wait an hour or so. The FTP site is only mirrored once every hour. I <I>have</I> uploaded them, and they will be there, I promise :-))<P>
 I never thought I'd see the day when *I* wrote *Armin*, and told him that a bug was fixed in QuArK !!
