<headline>What's .ACE ?</headline>
I have repacked the zhlt11.zip to zhlt11.ace using the <a target="_blank" href="http://www.winace.com">WinAce archiver</a>. It compresses
much better than <a target="_blank" href="http://www.winzip.com">WinZip</a> so prepare to see more archives of this type in the future.
Using WinAce archiver on zhlt11, it reduced it by 93KB. It might not be much for you T1 owners, but if you only got a 56K-modem and pay per
minute your online like I do, it does count!<br>
