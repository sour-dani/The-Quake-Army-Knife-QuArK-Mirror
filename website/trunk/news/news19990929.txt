Due to real life<sup>tm</sup>, Armin is moving to another country to continue his studies. I got this e-mail from him, send 1999.09.28:
<blockquote>
I am now readying myself to leave home... I'm leaving
tomorrow (wednesday). I won't release anything until then, but I hope I
will be able to finish my work on QuArK 5.11 and release it in a reasonable
amount of time... I am currently busy reorganizing a major part of the
texture and image object structure; this is needed by the most recent games
like KingPin and Quake 3 which use standard image files as textures. In the
previous versions of QuArK, textures and images where completely separated.
I guess all these changes will call for a second beta version...<br>

A bientôt,<br>

Armin.
