<headline>Zoner's Half-Life Tools v2.0.3</headline>
With the coming new version 3.3 of a rival map-editor (that is for Half-Life only), Zoner have made a new version (and a new, and then yet a new <img src="pics/SmileyMedium.gif" align=absmiddle width=15 height=15>) of his Half-Life Tools to support it and put in some additions too.
Here's a snip from his changelog:
<ul>
<li>A new program 'netvis' has been added to ZHLT. This program is for distributed VIS compilation across multiple computers.
<li>-skyclip is now obsolete. Clipping of skies, by automatically copying all SKY brushes with a duplicate CLIP brush in the same place, is now automatic and cannot be turned off.
<li>HLCSG will more actively find brushes of the wrong type in the world or in entities (i.e. CLIP, HINT, etc in entities)
</ul>
