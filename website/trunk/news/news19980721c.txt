<STRONG>QuArK Ring Maps Site</STRONG><BR>
<LI>The <a href="http://members.tripod.com/~qrm/" target="_parent">QuArK Ring-Map</a> is up! Join in the fun!<BR>
This site is a chain-map style site for users of the QuArK map editor only, with a slight difference. Instead of ONE map being passed around from mapper to mapper, each mapper works on his own copy of the map. Once a certain time period has passed, the maps are re-circulated, hence the ring-map concept.<BR><BR>
This could be a great way for new users to get their map kicked, inspected, and edited by more experienced users, and experienced users, well... they can get new ideas in their jaded noggins! :)
