<headline>QuArK 6.6.0 Beta 7 nightly released!</headline>

<p>Link: <a href="http://quark.sourceforge.net/downloads/release_zips/quark-win32-6.6.0Beta7Nightly6Dec2015.exe">http://quark.sourceforge.net/downloads/release_zips/quark-win32-6.6.0Beta7Nightly6Dec2015.exe</a></p>

<p>Please test it (if you want), and report any problems with the experimental workaround in this forum thread: <a href="http://quark.sourceforge.net/forums/index.php?topic=1080">http://quark.sourceforge.net/forums/index.php?topic=1080</a></p>
