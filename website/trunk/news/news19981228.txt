<p><a href="http://asp.planetquake.com/dl/dl.asp?quark/quark53.exe">QuArK 5.3 released !</a></p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Half-Life support.</strong> Ready and tested. Enjoy !
<li><strong>Sin support</strong> is included but untested yet. Maybe it works, maybe not.
<li>A <strong>Model editor</strong> is present but still very basic. Actually, you can't do anything.
<li>Several "modes" are available for the red rectangle that appears when you drag
    the mouse over the map. You can have the rectangle select polyhedrons (default),
    or entities, or turn the rectangle into a solid cube (handy to quickly make a lot
    of cubes). You can also have a line instead of a rectangle : all polyhedrons touching
    it will be cut in two parts, and you can also cut whole groups in two -- this is a
    handy way to create the mysterious "shared faces" in QuArK. This means that you can
    later move the plane where you've cut the group without having to move all the faces
    of all the polyhedrons that were cut in two !
<li>Better User Data panel (the place where you drop your prefabs in the map editor).
    Now you can reach the whole content of the New map items and the Texture browser
    toolboxes directly. There is also a button "textures currently used in map".
<li>Group view flags, as in QuArK 4.07 : hidden groups, ignored to build the map, etc. BTW,
    the menu option "quickplay on selection only" is back.
<li>Basic map validity check. It will automatically find and show you some of the most
    common errors : mixed face contents, brush entities with no attached polyhedron, etc.
<li>Surface flags (for Quake 2) are now also accessible from the menus.
<li>Well... there are other minor news and bug fixes, unlisted.
</ul>
