It's nice to hear, that someone won a map-designing price, using QuArK as the tool.<br>
But remember; it is not the <u>tool</u> that win prices. It is the person with the idea that, by using <u>a</u> tool and possibly many
different tools, gain prices, glory and respect for his or her creations. Map-making is an artform... like painting.
