<headline>QuArK 6.5.0 alpha 1 with Terrain Generator released !</headline>
<p>With a new Logo look, stepped up version plat toe, improved installer and the addition of its own <a href="http://quark.sourceforge.net/infobase/intro.terraingenerator.html"><b>Terrain Generator</b></a> QuArK practically <b>LEEPS</B> out of the box at you! ! !</p>

<p>Besides more improvements, corrections and added support for Half-Life 2, QuArK now sports an entirely <b>NEW</b> addition to its repertoire of tools and features that has been long over due. The <b>QuArK Terrain Generator !</b></p>

<p>This also brings with it, a whole new section to the <a href="http://quark.sourceforge.net/infobase/intro.terraingenerator.html">QuArK InfoBase documentation</a> covering every aspect of this <b>powerful</b> new extension for QuArK. Giving detailed instructions on its use, vivid screen shots to help you along the way and linked <b>F1</b> Hotkey InfoBase assistance, you will soon get acquainted with its features and abilities like an old friend!</p>

<p><b>These Include:</b></p>
<UL>
<LI>New <a href="http://quark.sourceforge.net/infobase/intro.terraingenerator.selection.html">Terrain Modes Toolbar</a> linked to the Infobase and documentation detail.
<LI>Contains three terrain Shape Builder patterns for making surfaces.
<LI>Converter for other imported terrains such as GenSurf and Nem's Tools.
<LI>Face Converters for top, bottom or (a new feature unknown to other terrain editors) <b>both faces for shape movement together.</b>
<LI>Adjacent Faces Selector tool for selecting other faces touching a single one.
<LI>Selector Dialog Input for variations of terrain movement and shaping
<LI>Basic Selector allows selections through all normal polys and entities.
<LI>With more feature additions and improvements to come! ! !
</UL>

<p>Some of the other improvements and additions to this most recent release are:</p>
<UL>
<LI>Added new link to the <a href="http://quark.ironfoot.co.uk/">QuArK Resource Center</a> on <b>QuArK's Help</b> menu for easy access!
<LI>Quake 2 and Half-Life 1 updated waypoint data types.
<LI>Added Infobase link and info for use of saving camera possessions.
<LI>Major updates and changes to the Heretic_II DataHr2.qrk file.
<LI>Updated Genesis3D and Hexen_II data.qrk files with added corrections.
<LI>Major updates and changes to the Quake_1 DataQ1.qrk file.
<LI>HL2 / Source engine content update.
<LI>Ability to display HL2 Materials.
<LI>Added HL2-Prefabs (custom addon).
<LI>Sounds support via ambient_generic and improved HL2 NPC_entities.
<LI>As well as many other Half-Life 2 support items. To many to mention here!
</UL>
