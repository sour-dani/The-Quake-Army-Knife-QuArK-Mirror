<headline>Please be patient!! QuArK will support Q3A at some time</headline>
So Quake-3:Arena is out, big deal. So Q3Radiant is out, whats the fuss?<br>
There's been many people, who've asked when Q3A support in QuArK is finished. Well, as some of you might <b>not</b> know, it
does takes a long time to implement support for a new game, because every game has its differences; let it be models, map-structure,
texture-formats, you name it. All I can say is, that Armin and crew are working at it, trying to decode whats available of information
about the Q3A <a href="help/glossary/index.html#.MAP">.MAP</a>-format and texture/shader stuff, so <u>please be patient</u>. Come back once
a week, to see if there are anything new.<br>
