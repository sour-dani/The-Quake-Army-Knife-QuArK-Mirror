<headline>Beware the combination of Counter-Strike 2 and AMD Anti-LAG</headline>

<p>There's a problem with the latest AMD videocard drivers. They introduce Counter-strike 2 support for the Anti-LAG feature, but it <a href="https://twitter.com/CounterStrike/status/1712875606776729832">triggers Valve's VAC</a>, resulting in you being banned. So turn Anti-LAG off, for now!</p>
