<headline>Forums netiquette</headline>
Please follow the suggested <a href="forums.php">abbreviations of games</a>, and put them in your subject-line, when writing to the QuArK forums.
This will make it easier for the reader, to get a better clue about your problem, when a paticular game can be linked to it.<br>
Another good idea, would be to read your own description of your problem twice, to see if others will understand it, and you haven't missed anything
that may be obivous to you, but not to the reader.<br>
