<headline>InstaPoll</headline>
Wow! If you ask the right questions, you sure get a lot of attention. <b>50%</b> of you would like to see Quake 3 Arena support in
a future version of QuArK, even though you all know, that Armin will work hard on Q3A support, when it becomes legal to do so.
<b>25%</b> would like to see Team Fortress 2 support, which is actually a lot more than I anticipated, due to the previous instapoll,
where only 10% makes TF style maps <img src="pics/SmileyMedium.gif" align=absmiddle width=15 height=15>. I do hope you guys are up for it, making TF2 maps, because it isn't going to be just some easy
(and boring) deathmatch maps the players want. <b>13%</b> would like to see Kingpin support. Well, what are you waiting for? Since
Kingpin is based upon the Quake-2 engine, it shouldn't be so hard to figure out. Those of you, who got the required knowledge and skills,
and a copy of the game, should help Armin by giving him the required specs of the files, their structure, placement etc., and you should
start collecting everything known of the entities in the game, so a data add-on can be made. Next up is Star Trek Voyager: Elite Force
with <b>5%</b>. I don't know much about this game, except its Star Trek with a Quake 3 warp-engine.. ehhh... graphics-engine!
The much hyped Daikatana only got <b>2%</b>? I haven't seen the demo myself, but is the game that bad? Or just another Quake-2 clone?
<b>1%</b> would like to see Soldier of Fortune support in a future version of QuArK. Well, the game sure looks like a feast of art (and gore!),
lets see what the gameplay brings.<br>
