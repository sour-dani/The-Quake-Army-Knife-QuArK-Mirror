<headline>Call of Duty: Warzone Mobile about to go offline</headline>

<p>Activision Blizzard is about to <a target="_blank" href="https://support.activision.com/warzone-mobile/articles/warzone-mobile-service-changes">kill Call of Duty: Warzone Mobile</a>. Because there we not enough players to Activision Blizzard liking, they are taking the servers offline on 17 April 17 2026, which means the game will no longer be playable. If you still have COD Points (their in-game currency worth real money), you have to spend it before that time, but the content you purchase with it will become useless on the set date anyway. So be quick to enjoy that valuable content!</p>
