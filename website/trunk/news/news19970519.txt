<DD><FONT SIZE=3 COLOR="#0080FF"><B>- Exploding Wall Addon v1.2 -</B></FONT><P>
<A HREF="mailto:alper@uhavax.hartford.edu">AnArcHISt</A> has released version 1.2 of his "Exploding Wall" QME Addon. New in this version is the ability to assign a trigger to it using target->targetname. This gives you the ability to make landmines etc. for your maps. Pretty cool feature. I mentioned that there was a "small" and a "large" explosion in this Addon, but I should tell you that the "small" explosion is perfect for small items such as cups, bottles, and other small items. It's more of a "smash" than an explosion, and should lead to some cool new items in your levels. I have updated this Addon to the new version on the <A HREF="addon.html">QME Addon Page</A>.
