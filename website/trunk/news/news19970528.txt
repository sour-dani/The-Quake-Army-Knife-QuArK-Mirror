<DD><FONT SIZE=3 COLOR="#0080FF"><B>- "User Created" levels -</B></FONT><P>
I got sent an e-mail from <A HREF="mailto:SwBthumper@aol.com">Steve Wilson</A> (aka: Thundermug), with an announcement that he's released 7 of his deathmatch levels (made with QuakeMap 2.8) and uploaded them to Walnut Creek (cdrom.com). I've added links to the whole bunch on the <A HREF="user.html">User Levels</A> Page. They are all fairly small, but fun levels that he put together for those of us who still like deathmatching over the modem (ie: not 'net). I think you'll like them. I especially recommend the "Outland" levels. The whole bunch is under 1.7 megs zipped in total (most are around 200k), so get them all. (The "Port" level kept crashing with Winquake, and wouldn't run at all with GLQuake, so you may have problems with it... but it looks fun too).
