<DD><FONT SIZE=3 COLOR="#0080FF"><B>- QuArK 3.4 soon -</B></FONT><P>
I received an e-mail from Armin today with news of what one of the additions to QuArK 3.4 will be. Included will be "Context Sensitive" help. Moving the mouse over a button etc. and pressing F1 will bring up a help item for that subject. The only part of QuArK that this is available for so far is the Map editor so far.<BR>
When Armin says "release soon" I expect it in a couple of days, so it may be available as early as tomorrow or Tuesday. (No, I don't know exactly when... I'll be watching this site for the news myself :-))
