<headline>Call of Duty co-creator Vince Zampella has died, aged 55</headline>

<p>Sad news in what should be cheerful times: Vince Zampella (aged 55) has <a target="_blank" href="https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cx25rled0ylo">died in what appears to be a one-sided car accident</a>. Additionally, another passenger also died in the crash.</p>

<p>Vince was one of the co-creators of Call of Duty, and he also worked on Medal of Honor, Titanfall, and Apex Legends. Later, he was involved with Battlefield 6, so his impact on our industry is large, and his legacy will be remembered.</p>

<p>May he rest in piece.</p>
