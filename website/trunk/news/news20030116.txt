<p>QuArK 6.3.0 (final) is finally released, you can pick it up from <a href="http://www.fileplanet.com/dl/dl.asp?/planetquake/quark/releases/quark_v6.3.0.EXE">fileplanet</a>, or see the <a href="http://quark.sourceforge.net/latest">latest</a> page for other options, including an update patch from the 6.3e provisional release.</p>

<p>Some of the main improvements over 6.3 are:</p>
<UL>
<LI>The problems with keeping vertexes on grid finally sorted out, and map formats for major editors (Valve 220 for Hammer, Brush Primitives for GtkRadiant) now written correctly.
<LI>Mitering option for the wall maker turns this duplicator into a serious tool for defining the volumes (rooms, corridors, outdoor areas etc.) of your map, in an UnrealEd-like style.
<LI>Power of many duplicators greatly enhanced with 'incrementing specifics' and flexible linear mappings: make a row of lamps, each with its own switch.
<LI>Now works well on dual monitors.
<LI>Numerous bugs, memory and resource leaks fixed.
<LI>New searches for microbrushes and other map problems.
<LI>Customizable hotkeys
<LI>BSP viewing for several more games
<LI>Most Q3-engine games now supported, at least to some extent. (Q3A, STVEF, SoF2, RTCW, JK2 fully, Mohaa partially.  Also Torque, 6DX and Crystal Space.)
</UL>

<p>There are also already some update plugins on the <a href="http://quark.sourceforge.net/latest">latest</a> page, with more on the way.</p>
