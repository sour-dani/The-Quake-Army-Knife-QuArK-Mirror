<p>After months of intense work, coding, searching, problem solving and testing the all new and improved QuArK 6.5.0 Beta 2.0 is making its début ! ! !</p>

<p>Many .. many .. many .. many fixes have been done.<br>
And a number of new features have been added as well.<br>
<b>With major improvements and additions  ! ! !</b><br>
Some of which are:</p>

<UL>
<LI><b><u>Map Editor</u></b>
<LI>Fixed the Software & Glide lock-up and smearing problems ! <b>YES !</b> They are <b>GONE !</b>
<LI>The addition of OpenGL lighting and transparency.
<LI>Fixed a whole load of memory leaks.
<LI>Changed Half-Life2 files to work with QuArK Beta using vmt files instead of vtf files.
<LI><b><u>Model Editor</u></b>
<LI>Improved quality of Model Editor's 3D Texture mode while dragging at increased drawing speed.
<LI>Completed the initial setup of the Model Editors Multi-fillmesh and color selection function.
<LI>Fixed the Skin-view to draw model mesh correctly.
<LI>Proper selection and display of skin textures for all model configurations.
</UL>
<p><b>WoW !</b> and that's only a small portion of the list ! 8-o</p>
