<b>Notice</b>: This is not an official QuArK release, but tiglari's own special version! Here's the readme.txt:
<blockquote>
<center>Features of Quark511qta</center><br>
This is a non-beta compilation of the quark511beta source,
with some additional features by QuickDeath, tiglari &
Alex Haarer:<br>
<ul>
<li>QuickDeath: saving maps to directories specified in a Quake
Context, not just paks.
<li>tiglari: new texture-positioning dialog; sliding things along
and around arbitrary axes.
<li>Alex Haarer: KingPin support (I've included his data files).
</ul>
The actual bugs in q511beta haven't been addressed, the ones I've
encountered so far don't seem to be show-stoppers (e.g. the
OpenGL bugs) and I don't know how to fix them anyway.<br>
<br>
This doesn't seem to work when extracted over a prior installation,
I haven't managed to figure out why yet, so unzip it into a fresh
Quark directory.<br>
<br>
&nbsp;tiglari
</blockquote>
