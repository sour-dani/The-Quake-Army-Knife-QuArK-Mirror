<headline>On Donner, On Blitzen .... On <b>BETA</b> ! ? ! ? ! ?</headline>

<p>HO_HO_HO! (as the BIG man says) Merry Christmas ! ! !<br>
That's right folks, <a href="http://sourceforge.net/project/showfiles.php?group_id=1181"><b>QuArK 6.5.0 Beta 1</b></a> has just been released!</p>

<p>Along with the joining of our newest member <b>DanielPharos</b> (or "Dancing Dan" as I call him), which we also wish to welcome to the QuArK Team!</p>

<p>Daniel is the one who has made the <b>Beta</b> possible by concentrating all of his efforts over the last month to <b>FULLY</b> implement <b>OpenGL</b> into the QuArK Map and Model Editors. And what a GREAT job he has done with it ! ! !</p>

<p>No more lockups or smearing of ANY of the 3D and 2D Textured views ! ! !</p>

<p>So when you get your new release go directly to the <b>Configuration... > General > 3D view</b> and select <b>OpenGL</b> !<br>
Then buckle up and get ready for the <b>Ride of your life!</b></p>

<p>Thank you Dan for that early Christmas present ! ;-)</p>

<p>Unfortunately, Dan is going to have to take a short break from QuArK until the beginning of the new year. 8-(<br>
<b>But</b>, he'll be back and with plans to add <b>DirectX 9</b> support and work on the Software renderer as well! 8-)<br>
OH! Did I mention that he also has <b>3DFX Glide</b> working too ?!<br>
<b>WOW!</b> What a trooper!</p>

<p>So, what else is new with this release?<br>
How about <b>MAJOR</b> improvements to the QuArK Model Editor ! 8-o</p>

<p>Yup! Model Skins are now displayed on the <b>Skin-view</b> page as well as vertex movement to re-locate the skin!<br>
It's not perfect... but it's a start.<br>
Even models without ANY skin will now load in anticipation of future development for the creation of custom models from scratch !<br>
<b>Bones</b> are also now displayed without crashing the editor when selected, but they do not align with the model...<b>YET</b>.<br>
But that too is in the works! ;-)</p>

<p>Lets see... What else ! ... Well !<br>
Many .. many .. many .. many fixes have been done.<br>
Some of which are:</p>
<UL>
<LI>Started a new Model Editor Infobase section and their direct function links from the Model Editor.
<LI>Added a new sub-menu to the Model Editors "Options" menu, with various methods of rotation in 3D views to choose from.
<LI>Added higher texture-size support (only works in OpenGL).
<LI>Changed Grid Scale displayed numbers to RED for better viewing in Textured 2D mode.
<LI>Up graded QuArK to use Python 2.4.4 for added future support development and expansion.
<LI>Fixed error and to display texture of bezier texture selection list change.
<LI>Fixed extruder 2D view to Unrestricted other items when view is closed.
<LI>Made a fix to stop filling multiple dropdown list with erroneous data, for example like a misc_model entity.
</UL>
<p><b>Shoo !</b> and that's not even half the list ! 8-o</p>
