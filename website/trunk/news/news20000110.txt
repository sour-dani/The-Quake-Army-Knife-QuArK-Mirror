<headline>The QuArK source-code now hosted at SourceForge</headline>
The Q3A support is still under development! However, Armin and crew now have a little faster way of merging their code fragments,
because the QuArK source-code is now put in a <a href="http://www.cyclic.com">CVS</a>-repository at <a href="http://sourceforge.net">SourceForge</a>.
If you don't know what SourceForge is, you better read their FAQ, but in short its a site that will host open-source projects, and have some tools available
so the management and control of the project becomes a little bit easier.<br>
