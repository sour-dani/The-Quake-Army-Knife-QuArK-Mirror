<BR>
There are only two real problems with the findMail mailing list system. The first (which isn't all that bad), is that everyone who wants to post a message needs to register their e-mail address with findMail.  findMail will not use it for spam of anykind, but they DO want to record you as a user.<BR>
Once that's set up, you're IN!  You don't have to specifically "subscribe" to the list to post to it.  If you WANT though, you can join the e-mail list and have all posts mailed directly to you!<BR>
<BR>
The other downer about findMail is that messages posted on findMail, don't actually APPEAR in the archive immediately, though they get mailed to everyone immediately.  This might actually be a big problem for a messageboard environement... if someone doesn't think their message posted, they might post over and over again... this I know from experience. ;) Also, people only on the web don't get information as quickly as a normal messageboard setup would allow.
