<DD><FONT SIZE=3 COLOR="#0080FF"><B>- New Addon QME -</B></FONT><P>
I've just finished my first sound Addon QME file. It's nothing super-special, it's just 4 new ambient sounds that I made while trying out CoolEdit. They did turn out rather well considering that I didn't know what the hell I was doing when I started. Now that I'm a little more comfortable with it, I think I'll be looking at making some more varied sound effects in the future. This addon contains 4 ambient sounds called "weird machine" sounds (original name huh?) They should add some spice to your levels, especially in a "high-tech" level setting. Instructions for use are in the "description" in the QME file. (Basically, just "link" and then "extract and compile"...that's all there is to it). <B>dmwmach.zip</B> (44k) is on the <A HREF="addon.html">QME Addon Files</A> Page.
