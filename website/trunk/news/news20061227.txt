<p>For those of you that already downloaded the new <a href="http://sourceforge.net/project/showfiles.php?group_id=1181"><b>QuArK6.5.0_Beta_1_H-L2_Fix</b></a> for <b>Half-Life 2</b> below, you'll want to go to our <a href="download_gamepaks.php"><b>NEW QuArK Game Paks... section</b></a> and get <b>BOTH</b> game support zips for <b>Half-Life 2 and Counter Strike</b> that will complete the process of <b>FULL</b> support.</p>
