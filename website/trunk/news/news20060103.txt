<headline>Welcome jfvg</headline>

<p>With the start of a new year, we expect many exciting things to happen for QuArK. One of which is the addition of our newest team member <b>jfvg</b>, better known as Jean Francois.</p>

<p>Already he has given us something to be excited for and proud of, a nice change for Half-Life 2 so that your custom textures can be viewed in the QuArK Texture Browser!</p>

<p>A user of QuArK since 1999, Jean has extensive experience with Half-Life 1, making his own mod with models, textures, maps, and coding along with other maps for Jedi-Knight.</p>

<p>With this latest change to the Delphi source code, that has been added for the next release, we wish to <b>welcome Jean aboard</b> the QuArK Team and wish him all the best and future success.</p>

<p>Remember <b>Keep your QuArK knife sharp!</b></p>
