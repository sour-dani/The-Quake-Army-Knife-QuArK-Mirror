<headline>Stop Killing Games: EU petition</headline>

<p>If you are in the EU, there's currently a petition happening that's asking lawmakers to re-consider the legality of publishers destroying video games they have sold to customers. In other words, if you bought a game, you should be able to play it, even if the makers decide to take the servers offline (or go bankrupt). You can find the website about this initiative <a target="_blank" href="https://www.stopkillinggames.com/">here</a>.</p>
