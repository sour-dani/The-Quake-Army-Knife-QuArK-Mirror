<headline>Another QuArK 6.6.0 Beta 7 nightly released!</headline>

<p>Another nightly version of QuArK has just been released! Compared to the 6 December nightly, it contains a workaround for an AMD driver bug, and it uses the latest version of NSIS which will work properly on hard disks with more than 4 TB of free space.</p>

<p>Link: <a href="http://quark.sourceforge.net/downloads/release_zips/quark-win32-6.6.0Beta7Nightly12Dec2015.exe">http://quark.sourceforge.net/downloads/release_zips/quark-win32-6.6.0Beta1Nightly12Dec2015.exe</a></p>

<p>Please post in the forums if you are experiencing problems with this nightly!</p>
