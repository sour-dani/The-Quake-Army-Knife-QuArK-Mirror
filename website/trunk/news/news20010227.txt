<headline>InstaPoll</headline>
<p>Its been a long time, since we last had an instapoll. Now something was brought up in the <a href="forums.php">QuArK-Python forum</a>, that 'Rowdy' suggested would be interesting to use as an survey/instapoll:</p>

<p>If you've noticed, QuArK has colorful gradient window-captions. The choice of colors depend on the type of window. The question is: Do you actually use this information, when switching between the QuArK windows, or do you think it is just a novelty?</p>

<p>BTW: In the <a href="http://quark.sourceforge.net/infobase/configuration/index.html">QuArK-configuration</a>, you have the choice of turning these colorful window-captions on or off. So if you don't see them, you've probably switched then off <span class="sml">(and can then vote for the 4th option <pic smiley_happy>)</span>.</p>

<span class="sml">(I started the instapoll yesterday, but hadn't the time then, to write a news-item about it, until today. Also I made an image, to show what 'colorful gradient window-captions' actually are.)</span>
