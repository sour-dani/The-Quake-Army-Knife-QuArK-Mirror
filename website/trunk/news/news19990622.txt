<headline>News from Armin about the soon to be released QuArK 5.10</headline>
QuArK 5.10 is becoming ready but it won't have killer new features apart for its much improved SiN
support, thanks to Tiglari. Other changes are small features or bug fixes:<br>
<ul>
<li>24-bit TGA files fully supported.
<li>Precision errors while working on vertices.
<li>Idem with linear duplicators.
<li>Dragging handles display coordinates in the small "hint" panel bottom left.
<li>Tim Smith's prism maker updated by Decker.
</ul>
