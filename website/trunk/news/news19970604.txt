<DD><FONT SIZE=3 COLOR="#0080FF"><B>- PlanetQuake mirror on Walnut Creek -</B></FONT><P>
Walnut Creek Cdrom, better known as "cdrom.com" has struck a deal with PlanetQuake (or the other way around) to mirror the downloadable files from PlanetQuake on the Walnut Creek sites. What this means for QuArK users, is that from now on, all the file links to QuArK, Addons, etc. will point to cdrom.com. In order for you, the user to take real advantage of this, check out the <A HREF="ftp://ftp.cdrom.com/pub/idgames2/README">README</A> file at cdrom.com for the location of the nearest mirror site to you. The files will reside in the " <B>/pub/idgames2/planetquake/quark/</B> " directory, and they will be available at all of cdrom.com's mirror sites. So, from now on, you can check out this directory for the newest QuArK related files on a site that is fast(er) for you no matter <I>where</I> you live !!
